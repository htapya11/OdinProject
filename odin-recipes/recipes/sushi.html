Sushi Recipe

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice, usually served with raw fish or other seafood, and sometimes vegetables, rolled in seaweed (nori) or served as bite-sized pieces.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Chicken, thinly sliced
  • Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado, carrot), thinly sliced or julienned
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Place a bamboo sushi mat on a clean surface and cover it with plastic wrap.
  2. Lay a sheet of nori, shiny side down, on the plastic wrap.
  3. Wet your hands with water and grab a handful of sushi rice. Spread the rice evenly over the nori, leaving about 1 inch of nori uncovered at the top edge.
  4. Arrange your choice of meat and vegetables horizontally across the center of the rice.
  5. Using the bamboo mat, roll the sushi away from you, applying gentle pressure to compact the ingredients into a tight cylinder. Lift the mat as you roll to help shape the sushi roll.
  6. Once rolled, use a sharp knife to slice the roll into 6-8 pieces.
